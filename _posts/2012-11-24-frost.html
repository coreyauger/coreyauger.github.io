A Modern Realtime Multiplayer Gaming Solution.

Game networking needs a smarter, more holistic approach for multiplayer, live game video and social graph interactivity.

FrostNet is taking a more innovative and modernized approach to multiplayer networking that combines conventional client-server gaming with interactive sharing of the live game experience between the players and their friends on the social graph. Every game from epic console battles to the mobile smash n' grab creates awesome content that is as valuable as the game play itself. We're here to help you share every awesome minute of your game with the world.

Welcome to the next level of game networking!

Cross-Platform

Target true cross-platform play across desktop and mobile via our FrostNet Client SDKs for popular game development platforms such as Unity, Project Anarchy, Marmalade and MonoGame, in addition to libraries for HTML5, .NET/Mono, iOS, Android and Windows 8 Phone.

Create multiplayer or second-screen gaming experiences that span desktop, mobile, and web browsers
