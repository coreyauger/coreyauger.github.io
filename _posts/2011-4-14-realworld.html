 AfterCAD InSite

Name: AfterCAD RealWorld
Language: C# .net, javascript, C/C++, Action Script
API: OpenGL
Platform: Windows, Linux

The First True Web 2.0 Viewer for 3D Models

A simple to use 3D document viewer that requires no plugins or downloads has arrived. AfterCAD RealWorld presents your 3D files in a unique AJAX driven, fast and simple to use environment. AfterCAD RealWorld serves your 3D documents to computers securely over the internet, allowing users to effortlessly zoom in, out, and around 3D drawings and markup, all with just a browser!

Features:

  • No Client Side Licenses.
  • No Downloads, Java or ActiveX Plugins to Install.
  • RealWorld works natively in Internet Explorer and Mozilla Firefox.
  • Full Zoom, Pan and Rotate views.
  • Markup, add comments, print and email current views.
  • Secure 3D data delivered as bitmap only.
  • Can be customized to meet your business specific requirements.
  • Supports ACAD DWG/DXF/DWF & Max.
  • DGN support coming soon.
  • Use HTML, XML & JavaScript to customize it yourself.
