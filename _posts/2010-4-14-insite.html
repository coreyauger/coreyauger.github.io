 AfterCAD InSite

Name: AfterCAD InSite
Language: C# .net, javascript, C/C++
API: OpenGL
Platform: Windows, Linux


Get up and running with InSite on demand.
Get Started

View and Mark up AutoCAD and Microstation Files with Just a Browser

A simple to use CAD Viewer that requires no plugins or downloads has arrived. AfterCAD InSite presents your DWG or DXF files in a unique AJAX driven, fast and simple to use environment. AfterCAD InSite serves your CAD documents out to computers securely over the internet, allowing users to effortlessly zoom in, out, and around drawings as well as control layers, markup and search, all with just a browser!

Features:

  • No downloads or plugins to install. The InSite CAD Viewer works natively in Internet Explorer and Mozilla Firefox on Windows and Linux platforms
  • Unique AJAX based technology allows you to navigate and mark up your AutoCAD or Microstation documents
  • Zoom, pan, add comments, draw lines and shapes, view layers, print, link to a document, and email a link - just some of the many features included with the InSite CAD Viewer
  • Can be customized to meet your business specific requirements

What does it do for me?

Lower the barrier to your data and services. Reach out to a broader client base and open up new revenue opportunities. Collaborate with an unlimited range of users; bring near professionals as well as consumers into your revenue base.

  • View, annotate and markup files with unparalleled ease.
  • Fully customizable and brandable to meet your requirements.
  • Offer new products and services to both new and existing clients.

How do I use it?

AfterCAD InSite can be purchased and installed on your web server and is easily integrated with your workflow. AfterCAD also offers InSite hosting for those who wish to take advantage of our expertise.

  • InSite works with diagram and smartlayer ticket URLs that are used to easily access diagrams and annotation layers.
  • Optional workflow tools available, including annotation back to DWG.
  • Available for Windows and Linux servers.
