Name: Adrenalin
Language: C/C++, C# .net, Action Script, Java Script
API: Direct X, WCF, LINQ, direct show
Platform: Windows

Press gamasutra

Remote Access to the 3D Games On Your PC. Adrenalin is optimized to let you remotely play the 3D games installed on your home PC through a web browser.

Webcast Your Games to Others. Adrenalin can broadcastlive game play from your PC to streaming services like Justin.tv

Share Your Games Live With Others. If you want, Adrenalin can allow remote users to play by giving them control of your 3D game.

Its Easy to Get Started!

  1. On Beta day, start a free Adrenalin account.
  2. Download and install the Adrenalin application on your gaming PC.
  3. Login to your Adrenalin account and fire up your games in a browser when you are away from home and need to play!

Optimized for OpenGL and DirectX Games.

Adrenalin has been tuned to stream and remote play any game you have on your PC. If your PC has enough grunt to play and display 3D games while you watch YouTube and other video content, Adrenalin should work great!
